HARRY POTTER

Harry Potter is a series of novels by British author J. K. Rowling. The novels follow Harry Potter, an 11-year-old boy who discovers he is the son of famous wizards and will attend Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. Harry learns of an entire society of wizards and witches.

The Harry Potter films are:-

  • Harry Potter and the Sorcerer's Stone
  • Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secret
  • Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban
  • Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire
  • Harry Potter and the Order of Phoenix
  • Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince
  • Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows- Part 1
  • Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows- Part 2
